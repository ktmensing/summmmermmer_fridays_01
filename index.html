Summmmermmer Fridays

isu.01

ideated on time, executed late



no ice cream sound

laden with past prejudice

covered in sweetness to perpetuate

sprinkled with nostalgia

no hidden meaning

or whitewashed words

atop a scaffolding of support

and empty air


no ice cream sound

dripping down our fingers

to pavement overheated

and boiling over

no ice cream sound

filling our ears with

false promises and

empty calories


refrains of:

everything will be ok

everything is fine

everything as accusation

everything will be great again


like sugar tainted memories

obscuring the toothache with

taste bur trickery

no ice cream sound  no  no  no ice cream sound
